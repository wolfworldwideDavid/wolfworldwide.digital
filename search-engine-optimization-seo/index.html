What is Link Building?

Essentially, it is the process of acquiring backlinks to your website. Backlinks are links from other websites that point to your site. When someone clicks through from the site being linked to your site, you get a boost in the SERPs.

Outbound linking

This is the process of sending links through social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Google+, and Instagram. There are numerous link building tools that focus on this type of link building, such as Buzzsumo, Ahrefs, and SEMrush.

Guest Blogging

This is the process of writing articles for other websites and then linking to your website from their articles.
Private Blog Networks
This is similar to guest blogging, but you can find influencers with high authority sites that are interested in linking to your blog.

Inbound Linking

This is the process of collecting links from other websites.

Reddit, Starting Pages and forums

Reddit and forums are great places to find link building opportunities.
